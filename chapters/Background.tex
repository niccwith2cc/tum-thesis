\section{Sampling Planner}

A sampling planner is currently being used to find possible trajectories for the vehicle to take, a cost function assess all the possible trajectories
and gives them a score depending on the path's "correctness". The path with the smallest cost is chosen and is then fed to the MPC controller as the ideal
required trajectory.
